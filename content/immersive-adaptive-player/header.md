## Description

The Immersive Adaptive Player is a web-based 360Â° video player with support to existing and novel adaptive DASH algorithms with audio prioritization, ambisonics support and accessibility features. This player extends the [ImAc Player](https://github.com/Fundacio-i2CAT/ImAc) developed in the EU H2020 [ImAc project](https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/761974).
